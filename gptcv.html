Vision & Mission


To become a centre of excellence in providing high quality and affordable technical education to rural students to develop them into competent professionals and entrepreneurs committed to the soil and society.


  • To provide industry ready technicians from rural areas.
  • To equip students to rise to the expectation of the society in their field of expertise.
  • To install ethical and moral values in students
  • To be part of the rural development by providing better education to all sections of the society.
